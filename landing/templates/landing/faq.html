Frequently Asked Questions

I don’t have a lot of experience with tech… can I do this?

Yes! FirstByte’s goal is to bring together educators of all backgrounds, and make technology more accessible for teachers and their students. We encourage curriculum authors to include guides for educators, to help clarify and expand on the information in notes and slideshows and provide solutions for classwork problems.

Do you provide the electronics supplies that go with your curricula?

Yes! We own various technology kits that you can borrow for free. Learn more about our loaner program here.

Who owns the curricula on FirstByte?

When you share your curricula on teachfirstbyte.org, you remain the owner of your content. Your material will be licensed under a Creative Commons license, which allows others to freely share and adapt your curricula as long as they give you credit and do not use the material for commercial purposes. View our Privacy Policy.

  • NonCommercial: You may not use the material for commercial purposes.
  • No additional restrictions: You may not apply legal terms or technological measures that legally restrict others from doing anything the license permits.

You are free to:

  • Share: copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format.

The uploader of curriculum cannot revoke these freedoms as long as you follow the license terms.
